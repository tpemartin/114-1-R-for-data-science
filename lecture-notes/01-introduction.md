## Slide 6: Setting Up Your R Coding Environment

* **Title:** Launching Google Colab with R
* **Instructions:**
    1.  Go to Google Colab: [https://colab.research.google.com/](https://colab.research.google.com/)
    2.  Create a **New Notebook**.
    3.  Go to **Runtime** > **Change runtime type**.
    4.  Under **Hardware accelerator**, select **None**.
    5.  Under **Runtime type**, select **R**.
    6.  Click **Save**. You should now have an R notebook!
